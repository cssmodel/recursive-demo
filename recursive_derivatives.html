Recursive Heaviside Function: Mixed Derivatives

Example of a second-order mixed derivative:

\[ \frac{\partial^2 u}{\partial \tau_2 \partial \tau_1} = - \delta_{N_1}(A) \cdot \delta_{N_2}(B) - \delta'(A) \cdot \tau_1 \cdot \delta_{N_2}(C) \cdot H_{N_2}(C) \]

Where:

\[ A = -t + \tau_1 H_{N_2}(B), \quad B = -t + \tau_2 \]
